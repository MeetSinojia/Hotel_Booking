Hot Air Balloon

Being Goa the most happening place in whole of India, its adventure trips are most sought after. Hot air ballooning is one such trip that give you an unforgettable experience in the sky. The hot balloon is a balloon carrying 5-6 passenger along with dedicated pilot. Hot Air Balloon ride is a recently started trip by tourism of Goa which will take you high in the air giving a chance to see 360 degree view of beautiful Goa, sparkling azure sea with the golden shores , hinterland, backwaters and lots more. Though the trip is fairly expensive and completely weather dependent, hot air balloon ride is already big hit and making itself one of the must to do trip in Goa. As you move up in the air from altitude to altitude the view you can capture with your camera is amazing. The cherishing this moments are a lifetime experience of air ride can be availed on pre-bookings through our website.
